Game Guide

Welcome to puzzle Fun! Are you ready for the brain excercise?


This is a classic Mystic Tiles Sliding Puzzle game. After click 'Start', number of the tiles will be shuffled randomly. To Solve the puzzle by arranging the titles to the original order (in numerical order from 1 to 8 for 3x3 puzzle, from 1 to 15 for 4x4 puzzle and from 1 to 24 for 5x5 puzzle, with an empty spot at the bottom right corner . You can move one tile each time and only to an adjacent empty spot.


Difficulty Level:

  • 3x3 puzzle: Easy
  • 4x4 puzzle: Medium
  • 5x5 puzzle: Hard

You will be timed when 'Start' button clicked but feel free to pause the game at any occasion.
