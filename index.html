Game Guide

Welcome to puzzel Fun! Are you ready for the brain excercise?


This is a classic Mystic Tiles Sliding Puzzle game. After click 'Start', number of the tiles will be shuffled randomly. To Solve the puzzle by arranging the titles to the original order (in numerical order from 1 to 8 for 3x3 puzzel, from 1 to 15 for 4x4 puzzel and from 1 to 24 for 5x5 puzzel, with an empty spot at the bottom right corner . You can move one tile each time and only to an adjacent empty spot.


Difficulty Level:

  • 3x3 Puzzel: Easy
  • 4x4 Puzzel: Medium
  • 5x5 Puzzel: Hard

You will be timed when 'Start' button clicked but feel free to pause the game at any occasion.
